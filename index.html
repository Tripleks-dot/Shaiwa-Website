About Us

About Shaiwa Homes

Jeffrey Shaiwa Homes was established in 2024 as one of the daughter companies of Jeffrey Shaiwa Holdings. Its primary mission is to provide practical solutions to the challenges of functional housing in Kenya and beyond. Guided by a vision to redefine modern living, the company is committed to delivering homes that combine affordability, durability, and aesthetic appeal. At the heart of Jeffrey Shaiwa Homes are values of integrity, innovation, and customer-centric service. By leveraging the expertise and resources of Gerizim Construction, the company guarantees high-quality builds that meet international standards while remaining accessible to the local market. Our pride is in our unique selling proposition: a seamless blend of design excellence, functional layouts, and community-focused developments. Clients benefit from transparent processes, flexible payment options, and after-sale support that fosters long-term trust. With a growing portfolio of completed projects and upcoming developments, Jeffrey Shaiwa Homes positions itself as a trusted partner for anyone seeking secure, modern, and value-driven housing solutions in Kenya and across the region.
